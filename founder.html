Meet the founder

Brittney Davis on why PJR Team exists—and what partnership means for healthcare organizations.

“I’ve spent over a decade in the healthcare industry focused on digital media, marketing and reputation management—and more than 20 years in marketing overall.”

“During that time, I’ve seen a lot, but more importantly, I’ve listened. Working closely with SNFs and ALFs, and healthcare companies in between, I’ve had the opportunity to hear firsthand where the gaps are—what’s needed, wanted, and what hasn’t been delivered.”

“That’s what led me to take the leap and build something different. At PJR, our goal is simple: to give our partners confidence. Confidence that they have a team behind them that truly supports, protects, and manages their digital presence, marketing needs and reputation.”

“We understand that healthcare providers are juggling a million priorities every day. If we can take one thing off their plate—one less stress, one less unknown—then we’re doing our job right.”

“By handling your full digital ecosystem, we free up your staff to focus on what matters most—your patients, the quality of care you deliver and your business.”

“I genuinely love what I do, and I’m incredibly proud of the team we’ve built at PJR.”

— Brittney Davis
